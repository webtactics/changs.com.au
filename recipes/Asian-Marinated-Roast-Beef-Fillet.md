ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 garlic cloves, finely grated or crushed</li>
  <li>1 kg beef fillet</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. vegetable oil</li>
  <li>250g <a href="/products/Changs-Vermicelli-Rice-Noodles/" title="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles" alt="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles">Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles</a></li>
  <li>&frac14; cup (60ml) Chang&rsquo;s Fish Sauce</li>
  <li>2 long red chillies, seeds removed, chopped</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup (55g) caster sugar</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup (60ml) rice vinegar</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup (60ml) lime juice</li>
  <li>75g roasted peanuts, chopped</li>
  <li>1 golden shallot or spring onion, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1/3 cup chopped coriander</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Marinade:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. Chang&rsquo;s Oyster Sauce</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. honey</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp. ginger, grated</li>
  <li>2 garlic cloves, finely grated or crushed</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Whisk soy, honey, sesame, ginger and half the garlic in a small bowl. Coat the beef in marinade, then refrigerate for two hours. Remove the meat from the refrigerator, and allow to warm to room temperature before cooking.</li>
  <li>Preheat oven to 190&deg;C. Heat the oil in a flameproof roasting pan in the stove over medium-high heat. Sear the beef for 1-2 minutes on each side.</li>
  <li>Transfer beef to the oven, and roast for 20-25 mins for medium-rare (or done until your liking).</li>
  <li>Rest beef, loosely covered, for 10 minutes and prepare salad.</li>
  <li>Soak noodles in boiling water according to packet instructions, then drain and place in a large bowl.</li>
  <li>Shake fish sauce, chilli, sugar, vinegar and lime juice in a screw top jar to combine. Toss half the dressing with noodles, shallot or spring onion, nuts and coriander.</li>
  <li>Slice the beef and serve with the noodles and sprouts, drizzled with the remaining dressing.</li>
  </ol>
