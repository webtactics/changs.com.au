ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 butterflied 2 &ndash; 2.5 kilo (before bone removed)leg of lamb, trimmed of excess fat</li>
  <li>1 thumb of ginger, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>6 cloves garlic</li>
  <li>Zest and juice of 1 lemon</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. brown sugar</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. dry mustard</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. sea salt</li>
  <li>2tbsp. Dijon mustard</li>
  <li>4 tbsp. neutral oil</li>
  <li>3 bunches of bokchoy or your favourite Chinese green vegetables</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine all ingredients except the lamb in a food processor and blend to a thin paste. Be sure to use a gluten-free mustard if necessary.</li>
  <li>Cover the lamb with the mixture and marinate, covered in the fridge for 4-12 hours.</li>
  <li>Bring back to room temperature and cook on the flat grill over medium/high heat for 10-15 minutes either side, basting with any remaining marinade until the last five minutes of cooking.</li>
  <li>Allow to rest for ten minutes loosely covered with foil while steaming your bokchoy or favourite Chinese green vegetables. Slice lamb and serve combined.</li>
  </ol>
