ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>6 boneless chicken thighs, trimmed of excess fat and cut into 2-3cm pieces</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>1 thumb ginger, peeled and grated</li>
  <li>3 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbsp. tomato ketchup</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 red capsicum, cut into 2cm pieces</li>
  <li>1 green capsicum, cut into 2cm pieces</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine all ingredients except spring onions in a bowl and marinate covered in the fridge for 2-4 hours.</li>
  <li>Thread the chicken and capsicum onto pre-soaked marinated skewers and cook over low/medium heat on the char grill until just cooked through.</li>
  <li>Serve with steamed rice and Chinese greens.</li>
  </ol>
