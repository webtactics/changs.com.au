ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>600g pork mince</li>
  <li>4 green onions, finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>2 tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>3 tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>1tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 egg, lightly whisked</li>
  <li>1/4 cup rice bran or vegetable oil</li>
  <li>100g mini mixed capsicums, sliced</li>
  <li>200g Swiss brown mushrooms, sliced</li>
  <li>150g oyster mushrooms, sliced</li>
  <li>150g cherry tomatoes</li>
  <li>1/2 small bunch watercress, washed, picked</li>
  <li>2x 100g packets <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine mince, onion, 1/2 the garlic, Oyster sauce, 1/2 the Sesame oil, Soy sauce and egg in a medium bowl until well mixed.</li>
  <li>Shape 1/4 cup portions into balls, flatten slightly.</li>
  <li>Heat the rice bran oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Cook meatballs, in batches, turning, for 4-5 minutes or until golden and cooked through.</li>
  <li>Transfer to a plate and add remaining garlic and Sesame oil to the frying pan with the capsicum, mushrooms and tomatoes.</li>
  <li>Stir fry 2-3 minutes or until mushrooms are softened.</li>
  <li>Return meatballs to pan with watercress and noodles and toss to combine.</li>
  </ol>
