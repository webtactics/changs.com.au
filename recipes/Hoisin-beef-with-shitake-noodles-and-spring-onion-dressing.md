ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>400g beef rump, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1/3 cup Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin sauce</li>
  <li>2 tbsps peanut oil</li>
  <li>1 large brown onion, sliced</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>250g shitake mushrooms, sliced</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Shelf-Fresh-Noodles-Hokkien-Style/" title="Chang's Hokkien Noodles" alt="Chang's Hokkien Noodles">Chang's Hokkien Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1/4 cup gluten-free beef stock</li>
  <li>1/2 cup thinly sliced spring onions</li>
  <li>1/4 cup firmly packed coriander leaves, finely chopped</li>
  <li>1 red chilli, finely chopped</li>
  <li>1/4 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Place beef and Hoisin sauce in a large bowl, stir until well combined.</li>
  <li>Heat half the peanut oil in a large frying pan or wok over high heat; add beef, in batches, and stir-fry 3-4 minutes or until golden and cooked through.</li>
  <li>Transfer to a plate.</li>
  <li>Add remaining oil to the pan and stir-fry brown onion, garlic and mushrooms for 2-3 minutes or until onions are browned and softened.</li>
  <li>Return beef to pan with stock and noodles and stir-fry a further 2-3 minutes or until combined.</li>
  <li>Serve garnished with the combined spring onion, coriander, chilli, Soy and sesame oil.</li>
  </ol>
  <p>Gluten Free Option: use Wok-Ready Noodles in place of Hokkien Noodles</p>
