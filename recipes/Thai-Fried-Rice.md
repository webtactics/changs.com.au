ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>4 cups cooked Jasmine rice, cold</li>
  <li>3 tablespoons <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tablespoon <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 teaspoons hot sauce</li>
  <li>300g firm tofu, patted dry and diced into 1cm cubes</li>
  <li>3 tablespoons cooking oil</li>
  <li>1 large brown onion, diced</li>
  <li>3 garlic cloves, minced</li>
  <li>3 green onions, sliced on the diagonal</li>
  <li>1 carrot, finely diced</li>
  <li>1 red capsicum, finely sliced and diced</li>
  <li>2 eggs, whisked</li>
  <li>Sea salt and pepper</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>To serve:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>Cucumber slices</li>
  <li>Mint leaves</li>
  <li>Chilli slices</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat oil in a large wok and over a high heat add the tofu and carrot. Stir fry for 4 minutes or until lightly golden. Add the onion, garlic and capsicum and stir fry for a further 2 minutes.</li>
  <li>Push the ingredients to the side and pour the eggs onto the other side and quickly scramble the eggs for about 30 seconds.</li>
  <li>Add the cold rice and combine well, then pour over the Tamari Light Soy Sauce, Fish Sauce and hot sauce. Stir fry for 2 minutes and finally add the green onions and season. Combining well.</li>
  <li>Serve garnishing each plate with mint leaves, cucumber slices, chilli slices and a lime wedge.</li>
  </ol>
