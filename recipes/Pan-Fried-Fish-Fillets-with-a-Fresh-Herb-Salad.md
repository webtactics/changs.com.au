ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>4 x 160-200 gram while fish fillets, bones removed</li>
  <li>½ cup Chang’s Oyster Sauce</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. plain flour</li>
  <li>2 spring onions, white and pale green parts only, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1 medium red chilli, seeds removed, thinly sliced lengthways</li>
  <li>1 cup coriander leaves</li>
  <li>1 cup basil or Thai basil leaves</li>
  <li>¼ cup sprigs of dill</li>
  <li>½ medium red chilli, cut into long fine strips - optional</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. fried shallots - optional</li>
  <li>2 tsp. extra virgin olive oil</li>
  <li>1 tsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp. rice vinegar</li>
  <li>Lime, quartered, to serve</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Marinate the fish for 20 minutes up to 12 hours in the Chang’s Oyster Sauce.</li>
  <li>Mix the olive oil, sesame oil, light soy sauce and rice vinegar in a bowl and add the coriander, basil and dill, tossing to coat.</li>
  <li>Wipe off excess oyster sauce from the fish and dust lightly in flour. Then fry the fish in the neutral oil in a frying pan over medium heat until just cooked.</li>
  <li>Place a piece of fish on each of four plates and top with the herb salad and the chilli and/or fried shallots if using. Place lime quarter beside the fish.</li>
  <li>For the gluten free, swap the plain flour for a gluten free variety.</li>
  </ol>
