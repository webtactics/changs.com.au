ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>&frac12; pkt <a href="/products/Changs-Vermicelli-Rice-Noodles/" title="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles" alt="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles">Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles</a>, boiled and drained OR 2 cups leftover cooked noodles</li>
  <li>185g can tuna, drained and flaked</li>
  <li>4 eggs, lightly beaten</li>
  <li>1 cup grated tasty cheese</li>
  <li>1 carrot, grated</li>
  <li>1/2 cup plain flour or gluten-free plain flour</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup corn kernels</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup spring onions, sliced</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup Thai mint or mint, chopped</li>
  <li>1 tbs chilli sauce</li>
  <li>Olive oil as needed</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Dipping Sauce:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>&frac12; cup Greek Yogurt</li>
  <li>1 tbs chilli sauce</li>
  <li>1 tbs fresh lime juice</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Using kitchen scissors, roughly cut cooked noodles into 4 cm lengths.</li>
  <li>In a bowl combine all ingredients and season with sea salt and pepper. Combine well.</li>
  <li>Coat bottom of frying pan with olive oil and add 3 heaped spoonsful of mixture into pan. Using the back of the spoon flatten the top. Cook for 2 minutes and then turn over and cook a further 2 minutes.</li>
  <li>Remove from pan and repeat process with remaining mixture. Add extra olive oil if needed.</li>
  <li>Serve warm or at room temperature with a cucumber and mint salad.</li>
  </ol>
