ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>500g minced pork</li>
  <li>1 large brown onion, finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 garlic cloves, crushed long fresh red chilli, finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 bunches English spinach, washed, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>2 tbsps Chang’s Fish sauce</li>
  <li>2/3 cup coconut water</li>
  <li>1/2 cup mint leaves</li>
  <li>1/4 cup Thai basil leaves</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1/3 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat oil in a wok over high heat until smoking. Add pork mince and cook, stirring to break up any lumps, until pork starts to brown.</li>
  <li>Add onion and garlic and cook whilst stirring for a further two minutes or until aromatic. Add spinach to the wok and stir until wilted.</li>
  <li>Add fish sauce, and coconut water and cook for a further two minutes.</li>
  <li>Remove wok from heat and stir in mint, basil leaves and Chang’s fried noodles. Serve immediately with lime wedges and shaved coconut if desired.</li>
  </ol>
