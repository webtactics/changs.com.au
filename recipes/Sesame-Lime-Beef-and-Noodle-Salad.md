ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>3 tsps <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>3 tsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsps sesame seeds</li>
  <li>1 tbsp finely grated lime rind</li>
  <li>750g beef rump steak, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>2 tbsps peanut oil</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>1 bunch (150g) Choy sum, trimmed, coarsely chopped</li>
  <li>200g broccolini, trimmed, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>1/2 cup vegetable stock</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Crunchy-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles">Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, seeds, rind and beef in a large bowl. Refrigerate, covered, for 20 minutes.</li>
  <li>Heat oil in a large wok until smoking. Cook beef, in batches, for 2 minutes or until browned. Remove from wok and set aside.</li>
  <li>Add garlic, and cook for 20 seconds. Add Choy sum, broccolini and stock and cook for 2-3 minutes until broccolini is tender.</li>
  <li>Return beef to wok and cook a further 2 minutes.</li>
  <li>Remove wok from heat and quickly toss through the noodles. Serve with chopped fresh chilli if desired.</li>
  </ol>
