ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 pack Changs Crunchy Noodles</li>
  <li>&frac12; small red cabbage</li>
  <li>1 carrot, cut into thin strips</li>
  <li>&frac12; red capsicum, cut into thin strips</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup coriander leaves</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup mint leaves</li>
  <li>&frac34; cup Changs Oriental Fried Noodle Salad Dressing</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Remove the core from the cabbage, discard. Roughly shred the leaves and place in a large bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the carrot, capsicum and herbs and mix.</li>
  <li>Just before serving add the Changs Crunchy Noodles and Changs Oriental Fried Noodle Salad Dressing. Toss until fully combined. Great served with chicken, meat or seafood.</li>
  </ol>
