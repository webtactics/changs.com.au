ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>6 hot dog bread buns, sliced in half</li>
  <li>6 hot dog sausages, prepared according to the packet</li>
  <li>1 carrot, grated</li>
  <li>3 radishes, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>Handful of sprouts</li>
  <li>Handful of <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> or <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a> for a gluten-free option</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Hot Chilli Mayo:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1/3 cup mayonnaise</li>
  <li>3 tbs sweet chilli sauce</li>
  <li>1 tbs lime juice</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Optional to garnish:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>Thai Mint</li>
  <li>Spring Onions</li>
  </ul>
  <p><em>Note: we call these Chang's Hot Dogs as they are dairy free and use our fried noodles instead of cheese for those with dairy sensitivities.</em></p>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Prepare hot dogs according to packet instructions.</li>
  <li>Prepare Hot Chilli Mayo.</li>
  <li>Open up each bread bun and spread the hot chilli mayo sauce over the inside of the bun.</li>
  <li>Evenly divide the carrot, radish, sprouts and hot dogs between the bed buns.</li>
  <li>Top with an extra drizzle of hot chilli sauce and a good sprinkle of Chang&rsquo;s Fried Noodles. Serve and enjoy.</li>
  </ol>
