ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 whole ham, bone in leg</li>
  <li>20 cloves</li>
  <li>2 oranges, sliced</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Glaze:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. rice vinegar</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. honey</li>
  <li>1 tsp. five spice powder</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine all the glaze ingredients in a medium bowl, and stir or whisk to combine.</li>
  <li>Carefully peel the skin from the ham and discard. Make 1cm deep cuts in a diamond pattern approximately 2-3cm apart all over the fat on the surface of the ham.</li>
  <li>Place a clove at the intersection of each cut. Place the ham into a large baking dish alongside the orange slices, adding one cup of water.</li>
  <li>Brush half the glaze all over the ham and bake in a hot (220 degree) oven for 15 minutes. Remove the ham, and brush with the remainder of the glaze. Bake for another 15 minutes.</li>
  <li>Serve ham warm from the oven, or cold from the refrigerator.</li>
  </ol>
