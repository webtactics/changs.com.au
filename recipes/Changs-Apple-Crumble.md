ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Apple Mixture:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>6 large green apples, peeled, cored and diced 2cm cubed</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons lemon juice</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon caster sugar</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup water</li>
  <li>&frac12; teaspoon cinnamon</li>
  <li>&frac12; teaspoon nutmeg</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Topping:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1 cup oats</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup brown sugar</li>
  <li>120g salted butter, melted</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Preheat oven to 160C fan forced.</li>
  <li>In a saucepan add the apple mixture ingredients and over medium heat bring to boil, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring frequently for 5 minutes or until apples are slightly softened. Pour the apple mixture into an oven safe baking dish.</li>
  <li>To make the topping, place the noodles in a food processor, and pulse until chopped but not pulverised (alternatively place into a food bag and using a rolling pin, break into small pieces). Next combine all the topping ingredients and mix well.</li>
  <li>Sprinkle topping over apple mixture and bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes.</li>
  <li>Allow sitting for 10 minutes, then serve with ice cream, custard, vanilla yogurt or on its own.</li>
  </ol>
