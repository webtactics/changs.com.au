ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Thai-Style-Rice-Noodles/" title="Chang's Thai Style Rice Noodles" alt="Chang's Thai Style Rice Noodles">Chang's Thai Style Rice Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>For Garlic Chilli Oil</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>2 teaspoons <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tablespoons <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>3 tablespoons vegetable oil</li>
  <li>3 cloves garlic finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 spring onion, green and white part sliced separately</li>
  <li>3 teaspoons red chilli flakes</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon Chinese black vinegar or substitute with rice vinegar</li>
  <li>1/2 teaspoon brown sugar</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Topping</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>green part of spring onion</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon toasted white sesame seed</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <h4>Preparing the Noodles:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Prepare noodles according to packet instructions.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Make the garlic chilli oil</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>In a heatproof bowl, add all the ingredients for the garlic chilli oil EXCEPT the vegetable oil.</li>
  <li>Heat the oil in a small pan until it starts to smoke and carefully pour the hot oil over all the ingredients in the bowl. Once the oil has settled down, give the chilli oil a stir.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Assembly</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Drain the noodles well and place it into a large bowl.</li>
  <li>Add the garlic chilli oil, chopped green part of the spring onion, and mix until the noodles are well coated. Sprinkle toasted white sesame seeds. Serve immediately while it's hot.</li>
  </ol>
