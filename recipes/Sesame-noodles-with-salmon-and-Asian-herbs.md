ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>200g packet <a href="/products/Changs-Egg-Noodles/" title="Chang's Egg Noodles" alt="Chang's Egg Noodles">Chang's Egg Noodles</a></li>
  <li>3 tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>3tbsps rice wine vinegar</li>
  <li>4tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>1tbsp finely grated ginger</li>
  <li>1 clove garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>2tbsps hulled tahini paste</li>
  <li>1 tbsp smooth peanut butter</li>
  <li>3 Lebanese cucumbers, seeds removed, finely sliced</li>
  <li>4 Atlantic skinless salmon fillets</li>
  <li>1 cup combined mint and coriander leaves</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Cook noodles according to directions on the packet; drain.</li>
  <li>Combine Soy, vinegar, oil, ginger, garlic, tahini and peanut butter together in a large bowl until smooth.</li>
  <li>Add noodles and cucumber and stir gently to coat noodles evenly.</li>
  <li>Heat an extra tablespoon of sesame oil in large frying pan over high heat, add salmon, skin side down and cook for 2 minutes or until crispy.</li>
  <li>Flip salmon over a cook a further 2 minutes or until cooked to your liking.</li>
  <li>Remove from pan, flake flesh and serve with the sesame noodles and herbs.</li>
  <li>Garnish with fried shallots and a squeeze of lime if desired.</li>
  </ol>
