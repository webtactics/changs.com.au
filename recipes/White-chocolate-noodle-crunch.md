serves: "10"
difficulty: "Easy"
author: "pwg"
ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>100g <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>Cooking spray</li>
  <li>1 cup white chocolate chips</li>
  <li>1 1/2 tablespoons milk</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon salted butter</li>
  <li>40g cranberries</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Coat a baking sheet with the noodles, spreading out evenly to the edges.</li>
  <li>Meanwhile, add the white chocolate chips, milk, and butter to a small saucepan over low heat. Stir constantly until completely melted.</li>
  <li>Pour the entire mixture over the tray of noodles, adding the cranberries.</li>
  <li>Let sit for about an hour at room temperature.</li>
  <li>Enjoy!</li>
  </ol>
