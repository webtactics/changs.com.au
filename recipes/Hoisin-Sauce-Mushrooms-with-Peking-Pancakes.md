ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>250g brown mushrooms, sliced</li>
  <li>250g enoki mushrooms, chopped</li>
  <li>1 tsp ginger minced</li>
  <li>1 tsp garlic minced</li>
  <li>1 tsp cooking oil</li>
  <li>4 tbs <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>&frac12; tsp Chinese Five Spice powder</li>
  </ul>
  <p> </p>
  <h3>To Serve:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 lebanese cucumber, thinly sliced into sticks</li>
  <li>1 green onion, shredded</li>
  <li>1 red chilli, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>Coriander</li>
  <li>Lime</li>
  <li>Peking duck style pancakes or Rice paper sheets</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Add cooking oil to wok and stir fry mushrooms for 2 minutes over high heat. Now add ginger, garlic and Chinese five spice. Stir fry for a further minute.</li>
  <li>Pour over mushrooms Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin Sauce and Sesame Oil. Bring to boil and then turn off heat.</li>
  <li>Heat pancakes according to packet instructions.</li>
  <li>Serve immediately to assemble pancakes. Place mushrooms on Peking pancakes, top with cucumber, green onion, chilli and coriander. Serve extra Hoisin as a dipping sauce.</li>
  </ol>
