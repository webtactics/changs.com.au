ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Shelf-Fresh-Noodles-Hokkien-Style/" title="Chang's Hokkien Noodles" alt="Chang's Hokkien Noodles">Chang's Hokkien Noodles</a></li>
  <li>200g leftover turkey meat, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. vegetable oil</li>
  <li>1 clove garlic, crushed or sliced</li>
  <li>&frac12; thumb ginger, peeled and grated or cut into matchsticks</li>
  <li>2 spring onions, white and pale green parts only, sliced</li>
  <li>&frac12; medium red chilli, sliced &ndash; optional</li>
  <li>2 shitake mushrooms, sliced</li>
  <li>4 stalks asparagus, cut into 3-4cm lengths</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat the oil in a wok, and add the garlic, ginger, spring onion, mushrooms and asparagus. Stir fry vegetable for 30 seconds, or until soft.</li>
  <li>Add the turkey meat, and stir fry for a further minute.</li>
  <li>Add the noodles, oyster and soy sauces, and cook for another minute.</li>
  </ol>
