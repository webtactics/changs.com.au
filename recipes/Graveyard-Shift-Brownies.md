ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 box brownie mix</li>
  <li>1 small jar of hazelnut spread</li>
  <li>4 large choc chip cookies, chopped up</li>
  <li>Store bought Halloween sugar decorations or sprinkles</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> (or <a title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" href="products/Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a> for a gluten-free option)</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Bake and prepare brownie mix as per instructions, set aside to cool.</li>
  <li>Once brownie has cooled, cut evenly into desired portion size.</li>
  <li>Layer with one generous teaspoon of hazelnut spread</li>
  <li>Sprinkle with chopped cookies and crispy noodles.</li>
  <li>Top with spooky Halloween sprinkles and decorations.</li>
  <li>For a savoury salted chocolate twist, sprinkle with salt flakes.</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong> Enjoy, ghouls and goblins!</strong></p>
