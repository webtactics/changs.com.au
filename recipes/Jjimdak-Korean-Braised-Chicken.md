ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>800g chicken, cut into medium-sized chunks (chicken drumettes, wingettes and thigh fillets)</li>
  <li>150g <em>Changs Wide Potato Glass Noodle</em> (half a packet)</li>
  <li>1 medium size potatoes</li>
  <li>1 carrots</li>
  <li>1/2 medium onion</li>
  <li>3 - 4 rehydrated dried shiitake or button mushrooms</li>
  <li>2 stalks of spring onion</li>
  <li>1 tsp sesame seeds</li>
  <li>4-5 dried whole red chillies</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Braising liquid (mix these in a bowl):</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>2 Tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  <li>1/2 cup of <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 Tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 Tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>1/4 tsp black pepper</li>
  <li>4 cloves of garlic, chopped</li>
  <li>1 tsp minced ginger</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Soak the potato glass noodles in hot water for about 45 minutes while preparing the other ingredients. Drain well before using.</li>
  <li>Clean the chicken and cut into small pieces. Trim off excess fat.</li>
  <li>Cut the potatoes in chunks (about 1-1/2 inch) and soak them in water while preparing the other vegetables. Cut the carrot, mushrooms, and onion into bite-sized pieces.</li>
  <li>Heat up a medium pot on medium-high heat, add 2 tbsp of vegetable oil and sear the chicken until it&rsquo;s browned on both sides.</li>
  <li>In the same pot, carefully add the braising liquid to the pot and bring it to boil.</li>
  <li>Add the potatoes, mushrooms, carrots, onions, dried chillies and 1/2 cup of water. Give it a good stir and cover, reduce heat to medium and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir occasionally.</li>
  <li>Add the glass noodles, and spring onions and simmer until the noodles are cooked (about 3 to 5 mins), covered. Stir lightly to make sure the glass noodles are mixed with the sauce.</li>
  <li>Garnish with the sesame seeds. (You can add additional spring onions for extra poppy colour before serving as well.) Serve hot with a bowl of steamed rice.</li>
  </ol>
