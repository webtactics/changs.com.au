ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Rice Paper Rolls:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>12 sheets rice paper rolls</li>
  <li>&frac12; packet <a href="/products/Changs-Vermicelli-Rice-Noodles/" title="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles" alt="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles">Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles</a>, boiled and drained</li>
  <li>12 coz lettuce leaves, cut into quarters (10 x 5cm)</li>
  <li>2 cups purple cabbage, finely shredded</li>
  <li>2 cups carrot, grated or finely julienned</li>
  <li>2 Lebanese cucumbers, sliced into sticks or julienned</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Crunchy-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles">Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles</a> or <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>Mint leaves</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Peanut Satay Sauce:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>&frac12; cup peanut butter (or nut butter of choice)</li>
  <li>2 garlic cloves, finely grated or minced</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tablespoons lime juice</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons maple syrup</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>1-2 tablespoons water</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Serve: Pickled Ginger</p>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Prepare all ingredients for rice paper rolls and arrange in bowls on a working surface.</li>
  <li>Fill a wide bowl with warm water. Dip a rice paper into the water, making sure to wet the whole sheet and then place it onto a working surface or chopping board.</li>
  <li>Arrange first a lettuce leaf, then a small handful of Vermicelli Rice Noodles and equal amounts of carrots, cucumber and cabbage. Place on top mint leaves and a couple of tablespoons of <a href="/products/Changs-Crunchy-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles">Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles</a> or Gluten Free Fried Noodles.</li>
  <li>Starting from the bottom, fold the rice paper over the vegetables, roll over and tuck in the sides. Complete by tightly rolling the rice paper into a roll.</li>
  <li>Place onto serving platter or slice in half to reveal colourful vegetables and place into serving bowl. Continue with remaining ingredients.</li>
  <li>To make the sauce, whisk together the ingredients and place into dipping bowl. Serve alongside Summer Rice Paper Rolls.</li>
  </ol>
