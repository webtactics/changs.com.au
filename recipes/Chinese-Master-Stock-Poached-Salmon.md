ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 x 500ml <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a></li>
  <li>500ml cold water</li>
  <li>4 x 180g Atlantic salmon fillets, skin on</li>
  <li>8 spring onions, sliced into 3cm lengths</li>
  <li>100g shitake mushrooms, sliced thinly</li>
  <li>1 cup bean sprouts</li>
  <li>1 small red chilli, finely sliced</li>
  <li>1 cup loosely packed coriander leaves</li>
  <li>1/4 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a> and water in a medium saucepan, bring to the boil over high heat.</li>
  <li>Add salmon fillets, spring onions and mushrooms, cover and bring back to the boil.</li>
  <li>Turn off the heat and let the salmon and vegetables sit in the stock for six minutes or until salmon fillets are cooked through.</li>
  <li>Meanwhile, combine bean sprouts, chilli, coriander and dressing in a small bowl.</li>
  <li>Divide salmon, spring onions and mushrooms between four shallow serving bowls or plates, ladle over a little of the hot stock and serve with the coriander salad.</li>
  </ol>
