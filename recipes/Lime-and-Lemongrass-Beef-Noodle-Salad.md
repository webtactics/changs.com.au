ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>800g beef rump steak, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1/4 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  <li>1 lemongrass stalk, white part only, finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>2 tsps finely grated lime rind</li>
  <li>2 tsps Chang&rsquo;s Fish Sauce</li>
  <li>2 tbsps peanut oil</li>
  <li>1 bunch chop sum, (150g), washed, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>1/4 cup vegetable stock</li>
  <li>1 packet Chang&rsquo;s Crunchy Noodles</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine beef, dressing, lemongrass, garlic, rind and fish sauce together in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate for 20mins.</li>
  <li>Heat peanut oil in large wok over high heat until smoking. Add the beef mixture, in batches, and cook, stirring for two minutes or until browned. Remove to small bowl.</li>
  <li>Add choy sum and stock to the wok and cook, stirring, a further two minutes or until choy sum is wilted. Return beef to wok and cook for a further minute to combine.</li>
  <li>Stir through noodles and serve with lime wedges, fresh chopped chilli and coriander if desired.</li>
  </ol>
