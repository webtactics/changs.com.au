ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Long-Life-Noodles/" title="Chang's Long Life Noodles" alt="Chang's Long Life Noodles">Chang's Long Life Noodles</a></li>
  <li>&frac14; small green cabbage, shredded</li>
  <li>&frac14; small red cabbage, shredded</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup fresh mint leaves</li>
  <li>1 large carrot, grated</li>
  <li>4 green onions, sliced diagonally</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons fresh lime juice</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>&frac12; cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a> + 2 tablespoons</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Cook <a href="/products/Changs-Long-Life-Noodles/" title="Chang's Long Life Noodles" alt="Chang's Long Life Noodles">Chang's Long Life Noodles</a> according to packet instructions, rinse under cold water and drain. Place into large serving bowl and coat the noodles with &frac12; a cup of <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a>. Toss to combine and set aside.</li>
  <li>Add all the salad ingredients to the noodles, including the cabbage, carrot, green onions and mint.</li>
  <li>Drizzle over the salad, <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a>, lime juice and the remaining Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing. Sprinkle the sesame seeds and toss gently to combine. Serve.</li>
  </ol>
