ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 packs <a href="/products/Changs-Super-Lo-Cal-Wide-Noodles/" title="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles" alt="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles">Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1 small head cauliflower, cut into florets</li>
  <li>2 Tbsp olive oil</li>
  <li>1 onion, finely chopped</li>
  <li>4 garlic cloves, minced</li>
  <li>1 tsp salt (or to taste)</li>
  <li>1/2 tsp black pepper</li>
  <li>1 cup of non-dairy milk</li>
  <li>2 tbsp miso paste</li>
  <li>Fresh parsley or chives for garnish (optional)</li>
  <li>Salt and black pepper to taste</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Rinse and drain the Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles thoroughly, then set them aside.</li>
  <li>In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Saut&eacute; the onions and garlic until the garlic is soft and fragrant, but not browned, about 1-2 minutes.</li>
  <li>Add non-dairy milk and bring it to a boil.</li>
  <li>Once boiled, add your cauliflower, and cook it until tender.</li>
  <li>Transfer the cauliflower mixture to a blender and add your miso paste. Cover the vent in your blender lid with a thin kitchen towel to allow venting and puree until the sauce is smooth and creamy, about 1-2 minutes. If the sauce starts to look dry, add a few drops of water or milk to adjust the consistency.</li>
  <li>Return the blended cauliflower Alfredo sauce to the skillet and heat it gently over low-medium heat. Adjust the sauce's thickness by adding more milk if needed. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.</li>
  <li>Toss the Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles with the cauliflower Alfredo sauce until heated.</li>
  <li>Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley or chives if desired.</li>
  </ol>
