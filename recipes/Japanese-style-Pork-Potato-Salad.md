ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>4 Asian cut pork cutlets, bone removed or 1 thick pork fillet, cut into 1 cm slices and</li>
  <li>slightly flattened</li>
  <li>1 cup plain flour</li>
  <li>1-2 eggs, beaten with a little cold water</li>
  <li>2 cups panko crumbs</li>
  <li>200ml Chang’s Hoisin sauce</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. white sesame seeds</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Potato Salad:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 large Desiree potato, peeled and cut in 1cm dice</li>
  <li>1 small cucumber, peeled (optional), seeds removed and cut into 1cm dice</li>
  <li>½ small red onion, very finely sliced</li>
  <li>½ cup mayonnaise</li>
  <li>Pinch salt</li>
  <li>1 tbsp.mirin</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Bring a pot of salted water to the boil.Add potatoes and carrots, continuing to cook until they are al dente.</li>
  <li>Add the cucumber and onion to the boiling water, and continue to cook for 30 seconds more.</li>
  <li>Drain and rinse vegetables under cold water, then set aside to cool.Mix through mayonnaise, mirin and salt and reserve.</li>
  <li>Next, crumb the pork by placing each slice in the flour, then egg, then panko crumbs and shallow fry in oil until golden brown. Once done, drain pork on paper towel.</li>
  <li>To finish, pour Chang’s Hoisin sauce over pork, before sprinkling with a few sesame seeds.</li>
  <li>Serve pork with the potato salad or very finely shredded cabbage and a lemon wedge.</li>
  </ol>
