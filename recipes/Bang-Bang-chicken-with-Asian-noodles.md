ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>750ml ( 3 cups) chicken stock</li>
  <li>125 ml ( 1/2 cup) Shaoxing wine</li>
  <li>4 cm piece ginger, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>3 large chicken breast fillets</li>
  <li>250g <a href="/products/Changs-Long-Life-Noodles/" title="Chang's Long Life Noodles" alt="Chang's Long Life Noodles">Chang's Long Life Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1/4 Chinese cabbage, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1/4 red cabbage, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>2 large carrots, cut into julienne or shredded</li>
  <li>125ml (1/2 cup) <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine stock, wine and ginger in a medium saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Add chicken, reduce heat to low and simmer 8-10 minutes until chicken is just cooked through.</li>
  <li>Remove from stock and set aside to cool slightly. Meanwhile, add noodles to stock and simmer for 2-3 minutes; drain and reserve noodles.</li>
  <li>Shred chicken and toss in a large bowl with cabbage, carrot, dressing and noodles.</li>
  <li>Serve garnished with toasted nuts, chilli, mint and extra dressing if desired.</li>
  </ol>
