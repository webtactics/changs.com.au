serves: "4"
difficulty: "Easy"
author: "pwg"
ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 pack <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1kg pork mince</li>
  <li>100g green beans, trimmed and shredded diagonally</li>
  <li>2 green onions, sliced diagonally</li>
  <li>&frac34; cup <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds, to serve</li>
  <li>8-10 iceberg lettuce leaves, trimmed, to serve</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat a little oil in a large wok or non-stick frying pan over high heat. Add pork and stir fry until browned and nearly cooked through.</li>
  <li>Add the beans and green onions and stir fry for 2-3 minutes then stir through the Changs Hoisin Sauce. Toss until the pork mixture is fully coated and cooked through then remove from the heat.</li>
  <li>Serve the pork mix in iceberg lettuce cup garnished with sesame seeds. Drizzle over a little extra Changs Hoisin Sauce if desired.</li>
  </ol>
