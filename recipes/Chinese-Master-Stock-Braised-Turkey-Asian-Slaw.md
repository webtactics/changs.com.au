  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 x 500ml <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a></li>
  <li>500ml cold water</li>
  <li>800g turkey breast</li>
  <li>1 large carrot, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1 small red onion, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1/4 small red cabbage, finely shredded</li>
  <li>1/4 small Chinese cabbage, finely shredded</li>
  <li>1 cup loosely packed mint leaves</li>
  <li>1/3 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  <li>1 x 100g packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a> and water in a large saucepan.</li>
  <li>Bring to the boil over medium heat, add the turkey breast, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.</li>
  <li>Turn off the heat and leave the turkey in the stock a further 15 minutes or until cooked through.</li>
  <li>Meanwhile combine carrot, onion, cabbages, mint and noodles in a large bowl, drizzle over dressing and mix gently to combine.</li>
  <li>Remove turkey from stock and slice thickly.</li>
  <li>Divide turkey between four serving plates or shallow bowls, ladle over a little of the hot stock and serve with the coleslaw.</li>
  </ol>
