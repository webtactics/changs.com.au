ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 x 500ml <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a></li>
  <li>500ml cold water</li>
  <li>1 bunch baby bok choy, thickly sliced</li>
  <li>1 bunch choy sum, thickly sliced</li>
  <li>140g snow peas, trimmed</li>
  <li>600g silken tofu, cut into 12 cubes</li>
  <li>1 small red chilli, finely sliced</li>
  <li>1 small green chilli, finely sliced</li>
  <li>1/2 cup red peanuts, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a> and water in large saucepan.</li>
  <li>Bring to the boil over high heat and add bok choy, choy sum, peas and tofu.</li>
  <li>Reduce heat, simmer for four-to-five minutes or until vegetables are just cooked through.</li>
  <li>Divide greens and tofu between four deep serving bowls and ladle over some of the hot stock.</li>
  <li>Serve topped with chilli, peanuts and drizzled with the sesame oil.</li>
  </ol>
