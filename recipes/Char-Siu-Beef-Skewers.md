ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1kg beef, sirloin steak or scotch fillet cubed</li>
  <li>3 garlic cloves, minced</li>
  <li>3 tbs <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>3 tbs <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbs <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbs <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>2 tbs Honey</li>
  <li>Cooking oil</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Optional accompanying Salad:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>Slices of cucumber</li>
  <li>Carrot sticks</li>
  <li>Bean sprouts</li>
  <li>Red and green onions</li>
  <li>Shredded Cabbage</li>
  <li><a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li><a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine all marinade ingredients in a bowl and add beef. Marinade overnight or a minimum of 8 hours.</li>
  <li>Thread beef through skewers.</li>
  <li>Brush oil onto grill or barbeque and over high heat cook 2-3 minutes per side (over 3 sides).</li>
  <li>Serve immediately. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and serve with salad. </li>
  </ol>
