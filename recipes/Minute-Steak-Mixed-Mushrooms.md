ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>4x 100-150 gram sirloin steaks, excess fat trimmed off</li>
  <li>150ml Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin Sauce</li>
  <li>4 tbsp. neutral oil</li>
  <li>1 clove garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>1 spring onion, finely sliced</li>
  <li>200g mixed Asian mushrooms, all cut into bite sized pieces</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Begin by marinating the steaks in 100ml of the Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin for 30 minutes.Remove the meat from the marinade.</li>
  <li>Heat a grill plate, BBQ or large frying pan, and brush with half the oil. Quickly grill the steak on both sides to your preferred level of doneness, turning only once.</li>
  <li>Rest meat for a few minutes on a plate and loosely cover with foil or a clean tea towel during the preparation of the mushrooms.</li>
  <li>Heat a wok, or large frying pan, and add the oil. When hot, add the mushrooms, garlic and spring onion, tossing only occasionally so the mushrooms brown. When nearly cooked, add the remaining Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin Sauce and toss to coat.</li>
  <li>Serve the steaks with a generous spoonful of the mushrooms and some <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a>, Wok Ready Gluten Free or Super Lo Cal Noodles twirled around a fork.</li>
  </ol>
