serves: "4"
preptime: "20 mins"
cookingtime: "30 mins"

ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 packets <a href="/products/Changs-Super-Lo-Cal-Wide-Noodles/" title="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles" alt="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles">Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles</a></li>
  <li>300g pork belly chopped into smaller pieces (substitute with sliced chicken thigh fillet)</li>
  <li>1 small onion finely chopped</li>
  <li>1 cup green cabbage chopped</li>
  <li>1 cup zucchini diced</li>
  <li>2 stalks spring onion finely chopped</li>
  <li>5 tablespoons Korean roasted black bean paste (Chunjang)</li>
  <li>3 tbsp vegetable oil</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp sugar</li>
  <li>1 cup of water</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Garnish:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>3/4 cups cucumber thinly sliced for garnish</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat and fry pork belly until cooked and browned on edges. Take out and set aside.</li>
  <li>In the same oiled pan, add onions and spring onions and fry until softened. Then add in zucchini and cabbage and fry until softened.</li>
  <li>Return the pork to the pan and add black bean paste, mixing well. Cook for 1 mins, stir constantly.</li>
  <li>Add 1 cup of water to the pan, mixing well. Bring to a boil and simmer until thickened. Seasoned with oyster sauce.</li>
  <li>Prepare noodles according to packet instructions, and strain well.</li>
  <li>Transfer noodles to a large serving bowl and pour sauce over noodles.</li>
  <li>Optionally, garnish with sliced cucumbers before serving.</li>
  </ol>
