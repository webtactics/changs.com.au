ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>4 duck marylands</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a title="Chang's Hoisin Sauce" href="products/Changs-Hoisin-Sauce/">Chang's Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. Shaosing rice wine</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. brown sugar</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed or finely grated</li>
  <li>1 tsp. five-spice powder</li>
  <li>2 cinnamon sticks</li>
  <li>24 large cherries</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine all ingredients except duck and cherries in a large, ceramic bowl. Add the duck marylands, stirring to coat well. Cover marinade, and refrigerate for 8-12 hours.</li>
  <li>Place marylands skin side up in a baking dish with the marinade, including cinnamon. Add one cup of water, and cover dish with foil.</li>
  <li>Cook for 45 minutes at 170 degrees. Remove foil, and add cherries to the baking dish, making sure they are covered with liquid. Cook for a further 20 minutes to crisp the duck skin.</li>
  <li>Transfer a Maryland portion and six cherries to four, shallow bowls and serve with steamed Asian greens and rice or noodles.</li>
  </ol>
