ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 pack of <a href="/products/Changs-Vermicelli-Rice-Noodles/" title="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles" alt="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles">Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles</a></li>
  <li>500g chicken (or pork) mince</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. vegetable, peanut or canola oil</li>
  <li>3 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbsp. rice vinegar</li>
  <li>1 tsp. sugar</li>
  <li>&frac12; tsp. salt</li>
  <li>1 bunch asparagus, tender parts only, cut into 1 cm pieces</li>
  <li>&frac12; medium red chilli, finely sliced - optional</li>
  <li>1 cup basil leaves, finely sliced</li>
  <li>1 cup coriander leaves, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>1 cup flat leaf parsley leaves, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>1 cup finely chopped spring onions-white part only</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Place noodles in a bowl and pour over boiling water. When just soft drain under cold water and reserve.</li>
  <li>Heat a wok and add the oil then the chicken mince and stir until it begins to colour. Add all other ingredients and stir until the meat is cooked.</li>
  <li>Transfer the noodles to a serving platter or shallow bowl and spoon over the chicken and herb stir fry.</li>
  </ol>
