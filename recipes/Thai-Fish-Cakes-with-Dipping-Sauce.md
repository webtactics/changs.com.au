ingredients: |
  <h3>Thai Fish Cakes:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>500g white fish fillets, diced</li>
  <li>1 red onion, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tablespoons red curry paste</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon caster sugar</li>
  <li>1 tablespoon lime juice</li>
  <li>12 green beans, finely sliced</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup rice flour</li>
  <li>Freshly ground pepper</li>
  <li>Cooking oil for frying</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Dipping Sauce:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 tablespoon<a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tablespoon <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tablespoon caster sugar</li>
  <li>1 teaspoon dried chilli flakes</li>
  <li>2 fresh garlic cloves, minced</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup crushed cashews or peanuts</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup fresh lime juice</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>To prepare the dipping sauce, add all ingredients to a jar and shake until well combined. Transfer to a bowl, cover and set aside.</li>
  <li>Place fish, red onion, fish sauce, curry paste, caster sugar and lime juice in a food processor and combine until paste-like.</li>
  <li>Transfer to a bowl and add the rice flour and finely sliced green beans. Combine well. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.</li>
  <li>With damp hands or using a spoon, create patties using a heaped tablespoon at a time, making them approximately 1cm thick.</li>
  <li>Add to a wide non-stick pan the oil and over medium heat place the patties. Cook 2 minutes each side or until golden brown. Repeat with remaining mixture adding more oil to pan if required.</li>
  <li>Serve with Dipping Sauce and garnish with fresh coriander leaves and wedges of lime. </li>
  </ol>
