author: "pwg"
ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>400g milk chocolate, melted</li>
  <li>3 pkt <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> or <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a> for a gluten-free option</li>
  <li>&frac34; cup hundreds and thousands</li>
  <li>200g Nestle Top &lsquo;n&rsquo; Fill caramel filling</li>
  <li>16 muffin cases</li>
  <li>100g (1 pkt) Mini decoration marshmallows</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine in a wide bowl the melted chocolate and noodles until well coated then pour over the hundreds and thousands and stir to evenly distribute.</li>
  <li>Place muffin cases into muffin tins and evenly divide noodle mixture. Press with a spoon the noodle mixture around the case edges, leaving an indent to pour the caramel once nest has set.</li>
  <li>Place into fridge for 30 minutes and allow nest to set.</li>
  <li>Remove from paper cases and add a heaped teaspoon of caramel into each nest and top with mini marshmallows.</li>
  <li>Serve immediately or place back into the fridge until needed.</li>
  </ol>
