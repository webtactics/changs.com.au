ingredients: |
  <p>A rich, creamy, umami-packed bowl layered with silky noodles, caramelised mushroom &lsquo;mince&rsquo;, and a fragrant chilli oil finish.</p>
  <p>Deeply comforting with just the right amount of heat.</p>
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Mushroom Broth:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>1 packet dried shiitake mushrooms (250g)</li>
  <li>2 litres cold water</li>
  <li>4 slices fresh ginger</li>
  <li>&frac14; can coconut milk</li>
  <li>1 tsp <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Mushroom &lsquo;Mince&rsquo;:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>Rehydrated shiitake mushrooms, diced</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a> (omit for vegetarian)</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Sizzling Chilli Oil:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>1 tbsp chilli flakes</li>
  <li>1 tbsp minced ginger</li>
  <li>1 spring onion, finely chopped</li>
  <li>3&ndash;4 tbsp neutral oil</li>
  <li>Pinch of salt</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Noodles:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li><a href="/products/Changs-Long-Life-Noodles/" title="Chang's Long Life Noodles" alt="Chang's Long Life Noodles">Chang's Long Life Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <h4>Mushroom Broth:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Soak the dried shiitake mushrooms in cold water for a minimum of 2 hours (up to 24 hours).</li>
  <li>Strain the soaking liquid into a large saucepan (this is your mushroom broth). Reserve the mushrooms.</li>
  <li>Add the sliced ginger and simmer for 10 minutes.</li>
  <li>Stir in the coconut milk and Chang&rsquo;s Tamari Light Soy Sauce.</li>
  <li>Keep warm.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Mushroom Broth -Shortcut Option:</h4>
  <p>Use a high-quality vegetable broth in place of the mushroom soaking liquid. If using this option, substitute with 250g fresh mushrooms for the mushroom &lsquo;mince&rsquo;.</p>
  <h4>Mushroom &lsquo;Mince&rsquo;:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat a pan over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Add the diced shiitake mushrooms and cook until lightly golden.</li>
  <li>Add Chang&rsquo;s Tamari Light Soy Sauce, Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin Sauce, and Chang&rsquo;s Oyster Sauce.</li>
  <li>Cook until glossy and slightly caramelised.</li>
  <li>Set aside.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Sizzling Chilli Oil:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine chilli flakes, ginger, and spring onion in a heatproof bowl.</li>
  <li>Heat the oil until hot and shimmering.</li>
  <li>Carefully pour the hot oil over the mixture.</li>
  <li>Stir and set aside.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Noodles:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Cook 1 bundle per serve according to packet instructions.</li>
  <li>Drain and divide into serving bowls.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>To Assemble:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Ladle the creamy mushroom broth over the noodles.</li>
  <li>Top with the mushroom &lsquo;mince&rsquo;.</li>
  <li>Drizzle with <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a> and the sizzling chilli oil.</li>
  <li>Finish with chopped coriander.</li>
  </ol>
