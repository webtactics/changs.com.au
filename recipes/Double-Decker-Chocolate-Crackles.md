ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Milk Chocolate Crackles</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>200g milk chocolate, finely chopped</li>
  <li>60g butter, chopped</li>
  <li>200g white marshmallows</li>
  <li>3 cups Chang&rsquo;s Crunchy Noodles</li>
  <li>1 cup Rice Bubbles</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup Dollar Sweets Magic Jurassic Bitz</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>White Chocolate Crackles</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>200g white chocolate, finely chopped</li>
  <li>60g butter, chopped</li>
  <li>200g white marshmallows</li>
  <li>3 cups Chang&rsquo;s Crunchy Noodles</li>
  <li>1 cup Rice Bubbles</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat milk chocolate, butter and marshmallow in a small saucepan over low heat until melted and smooth.</li>
  <li>Combine melted milk chocolate in a large bowl with noodles, rice bubbles and Dollar Sweets.</li>
  <li>Spoon mixture into a greased and lined 16 by 26cm slice pan, pressing down firmly with the back of a spoon. Set this mixture aside.</li>
  <li>Repeat the above steps with white chocolate.</li>
  <li>Spoon white chocolate mixture over the milk chocolate layer, pressing down firmly with the back of a spoon.</li>
  <li>Refrigerate for 1 hour, or until firm.</li>
  <li>Remove crackle from pan, and cut into 12 pieces.</li>
  </ol>
