ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>500g mixed, different types and coloured tomatoes, cut in different sizes and shapes</li>
  <li>2 spring onions, white and pale green parts only, sliced</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup coriander leaves, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>1 pack Chang&rsquo;s Original Crispy Noodles</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup Chang&rsquo;s Asian Salad Dressing</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup olive oil</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Mix the Chang&rsquo;s Asian Salad Dressing and oil in a salad bowl then add the tomatoes, spring onions, and coriander, stirring well to coat. Just before serving stir through the <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a>.</li>
  </ol>
