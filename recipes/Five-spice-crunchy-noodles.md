ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 pack Changs Crunchy Noodles</li>
  <li>1-2 teaspoons Chinese five spice powder</li>
  <li>1/3 cup Changs Hoisin Sauce, to serve</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Place the Changs Crunchy Noodles in a bowl and toss with the five spice powder.</li>
  <li>Season with salt and cracked black pepper</li>
  </ol>
  <p>Great party snack served with Changs Hoisin Sauce</p>
  <p><strong>Tip</strong><br /> As an alternative to five spice powder, try tossing Changs Crunchy Noodles with a little chilli powder or smoky paprika.</p>
