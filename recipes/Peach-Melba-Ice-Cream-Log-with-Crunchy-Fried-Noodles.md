ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Ice Cream:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>2 litres vanilla ice cream</li>
  <li>2 peaches, very ripe, diced into 1cm cubed</li>
  <li>1 punnet raspberries, washed and cut into quarters</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Decorating:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>Ready-made whipped cream</li>
  <li>1 peach, firm, sliced into wedges</li>
  <li>1 punnet raspberries</li>
  <li>&frac12; packet <a title="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles" href="products/Changs-Crunchy-Fried-Noodles/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>Mint leaves</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Remove ice cream from freezer and soften at room temperature for 10 minutes. In the meantime, line a 2-litre loaf tin with 2 layers of plastic wrap, leaving plenty overhanging the sides.</li>
  <li>Prepare the ripe peaches by peeling the skin and dicing into 1cm cubed pieces. Wash, pat dry and slice the raspberries. Optional to use a fork and simply squash them to create a &lsquo;sauce&rsquo; texture.</li>
  <li>Scoop 2/3 of the softened ice cream into the base and sides of the loaf tin. Smooth to create an even layer.</li>
  <li>Pour into the tin the peaches and the raspberries and then top them with the remaining ice cream. Smooth the surface.</li>
  <li>Fold the overhanging plastic wrap over to enclose the ice cream and freeze overnight to set.</li>
  <li>Remove from freezer. Leave at room temperature for 2 minutes. In the meantime, prepare decorations.</li>
  <li>Gently ease the sides away to release the ice cream and then turn upside down onto serving platter. Lift tin and peel back plastic.</li>
  <li>Pipe cream along the top, and decorate with peaches, fresh raspberries, mint leaves and Chang&rsquo;s Crunchy Noodles. Serve immediately.</li>
  </ol>
