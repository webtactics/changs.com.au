ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Base:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>300g peanut butter</li>
  <li>130g unsalted butter</li>
  <li>100g icing sugar</li>
  <li>150g mini marshmallows</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> or <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Top Layer:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>360g chocolate melts/baking chocolate</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons peanut butter</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Spray and line 18x18cm baking tray.</li>
  <li>In a pot gently heat the butter, peanut butter and icing sugar.</li>
  <li>Remove from heat, allow to cool for 5 minutes and then stir in half of the marshmallows and half of the Fried Noodles.</li>
  <li>Press the mixture into the baking tray and evenly spreading out, then evenly distribute over the top the remaining marshmallows and Fried Noodles.</li>
  <li>Melt the chocolate along with the peanut butter either in the microwave at 30-second increments or over a Bain Marie. Once melted pour and spread evenly over the base.</li>
  <li>Refrigerate for 6 hours or overnight before slicing into squares. Store in the fridge in an air tight container.</li>
  </ol>
  <p>TIP: Make them festive by sprinkling broken up candy cane or sprinkles over the chocolate layer while it is still warm.</p>
