ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>4 tbsps rice bran oil</li>
  <li>500g beef stir fry strips</li>
  <li>1 large red onion, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, crushed</li>
  <li>1tsp cracked black pepper</li>
  <li>1 bunch snake beans, sliced</li>
  <li>1 bunch baby bok choy, sliced</li>
  <li>3tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbsp raw sugar</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Hoisin-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce">Chang's Original Hoisin Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2x100g packets <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> li&gt;</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat a wok over high heat, add 1/2 the oil and beef strips, in batches, for 2 minutes.</li>
  <li>Transfer to a plate. Heat remaining oil in the wok and add onion, garlic and pepper, stir fry for 2 minutes or until, onion is softened. Add beans and bok choy to the wok and stir fry a further 3 minutes.</li>
  <li>Combine soy sauce, sugar and hoisin sauce in the wok with 2tbsps cold water and toss to combine well.</li>
  <li>Return beef to the wok with the noodles, tossing again to combine and serve garnished with lime wedges, sliced red chilli and fried shallots if desired.</li>
  </ol>
