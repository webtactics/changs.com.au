ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 tsp ground Chinese five spice</li>
  <li>2 tsps ground cumin</li>
  <li>2 tbsps Chang&rsquo;s Hoisin Sauce</li>
  <li>2 tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>4 x 200g skinless chicken breast fillets</li>
  <li>11/2 cups couscous</li>
  <li>250g mixed medley tomatoes or cherry tomatoes, chopped</li>
  <li>2/3 cup mint leaves, torn</li>
  <li>2/3 cup coriander leaves</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1/2 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine five spice, cumin, sauce, oil and chicken in a large bowl and mix well to combine. Refrigerate, covered, for 20 minutes.</li>
  <li>Heat a chargrill pan over medium-high heat and cook the chicken for 5 minutes each side until golden and cooked through. Set aside and keep warm.</li>
  <li>Cook couscous according to directions on packet. Combine couscous, tomatoes, mint, coriander and noodles together in a large bowl. Serve with the chicken and drizzle with dressing just before serving.</li>
  </ol>
