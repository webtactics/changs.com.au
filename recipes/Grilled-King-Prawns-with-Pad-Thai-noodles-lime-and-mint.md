ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1.2 kg green king prawns, about 12 large, split lengthways deveined, heads discarded</li>
  <li>2/3 cup Chang&rsquo;s Asian salad dressing</li>
  <li>2 tbsps <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsps garlic chives</li>
  <li>1/2 daikon, julienned, placed in a bowl of cold water</li>
  <li>1/2 bunch radishes, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1/2 iceberg lettuce, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>2 green onions or shallots, sliced thinly</li>
  <li>1 x 250g packet Changs Pad Thai noodles, prepared according to directions on packet</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine 2tbsps of the dressing with the sesame oil and brush over prawns.</li>
  <li>Heat a char grill pan or bbq over high heat and cook prawns, flesh side-down.</li>
  <li>Cook in batches for 2-3 minutes or until browned and just cooked through, turning halfway.</li>
  <li>Remove from pan, scatter with chives and serve with combined daikon, radish, lettuce, onion, noodles and remaining dressing.</li>
  <li>Serve topped with mint leaves and lime wedges if desired.</li>
  </ol>
  <p>You can swap out the pork mince for lamb or chicken mince if desired.</p>
