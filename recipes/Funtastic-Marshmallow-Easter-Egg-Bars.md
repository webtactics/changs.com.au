ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>120g butter, chopped</li>
  <li>200g white marshmallows</li>
  <li>2 packets Chang’s Fried Noodles</li>
  <li>2 cups Froot Loops cereal</li>
  <li>32 small chocolate Easter eggs to decorate</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Place butter and marshmallows into a medium saucepan. Cook, stirring over medium heat, until mixture is melted and smooth.</li>
  <li>Transfer to a large bowl. Add noodles and cereal, stir well to combine.</li>
  <li>Spoon into a 25 by 30cm slab pan, lined with baking paper. Allow to cool for 20 minutes, then while still warm press Easter Eggs into the top of the mixture.</li>
  <li>Refrigerate for 30 minutes, or until firm.</li>
  <li>Cut into 16 bars.</li>
  </ol>
