  <p>A fresh, fragrant, and deeply comforting bowl inspired by Vietnamese soups and b&uacute;n chả.</p>
  <p>This recipe comes together in just 20 minutes, with <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a> bringing an irresistible salty, savoury depth to both the broth and the meatballs.</p>
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Pork Meatballs:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>500g pork mince</li>
  <li>1 tbsp freshly grated ginger</li>
  <li>4 spring onions, finely sliced</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp white sugar</li>
  <li>Neutral cooking oil</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>For the Broth:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>1 litre good quality chicken broth</li>
  <li>1 carrot, cut into thin slivers</li>
  <li>4 spring onions, finely sliced</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>For the Noodles:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>2 x <a href="/products/Changs-Super-Lo-Cal-Wide-Noodles/" title="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles" alt="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles">Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <h4>Pork Meatballs:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine the pork mince, grated ginger, spring onion, fish sauce, and sugar in a bowl.</li>
  <li>Mix well until fully combined.</li>
  <li>Roll the mixture into evenly sized meatballs.</li>
  <li>Heat neutral oil in a pan over medium heat.</li>
  <li>Fry the meatballs until golden brown on all sides and cooked through. Set aside.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Broth:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine the pork mince, grated ginger, spring onion, fish sauce, and sugar in a bowl.</li>
  <li>Bring the chicken broth to a gentle simmer.</li>
  <li>Add the carrot and cook for 5&ndash;7 minutes, until just tender.</li>
  <li>Stir in the fish sauce.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Noodles:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Prepare the noodles according to packet instructions.</li>
  <li>Drain and rinse under cold water.</li>
  <li>Divide evenly between serving bowls.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>To Assemble:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Add the prepared noodles to serving bowls.</li>
  <li>Ladle the hot broth over the noodles.</li>
  <li>Top with the golden pork meatballs.</li>
  <li>Finish with fresh coriander, a squeeze of lime, and crushed peanuts.</li>
  </ol>
