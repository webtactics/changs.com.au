ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 chicken thighs, excess fat and skin removed, cut into 2cm pieces</li>
  <li>20 very thin slices streaky bacon, halved</li>
  <li>6 thick spring onions, white and pale green parts only</li>
  <li>4 thick spears asparagus</li>
  <li>8 small button mushrooms or 6 medium, halved</li>
  <li>5 tbsp. Chang’s Hoisin sauce</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Marinate the chicken in two tablespoons of Hoisin sauce for 2-12 hours, covered in the fridge.</li>
  
  <li>Once ready, wrap the bacon around the chicken, and alternate with spring onions on soaked wooden skewers.</li>
  
  <li>Heat the remaining Hoisin sauce with the Soy sauce, Sesame Oil and two tablespoons of water in a small saucepan and reserve.</li>
  
  <li>Cook the skewers over medium heat on a BBQ or grill until just done. Transfer to platters and spoon over the remaining sauce to serve.</li>
  </ol>
