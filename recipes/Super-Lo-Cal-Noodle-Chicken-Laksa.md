ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Laksa Broth:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>1 Tbsp vegetable oil</li>
  <li>1 onion, finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 garlic cloves, minced</li>
  <li>1-inch piece of ginger, grated</li>
  <li>4 Tbsps laksa paste (store-bought or homemade)</li>
  <li>2 cups vegetable broth (low-sodium)</li>
  <li>1/2 can (200ml) light coconut milk</li>
  <li>1 Tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a> (low-sodium)</li>
  <li>1 tsp brown sugar (optional)</li>
  <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Laksa Toppings:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>200g chicken thigh (or tofu for a vegetarian option)</li>
  <li>200g bean sprouts</li>
  <li>2 packets <a href="/products/Changs-Super-Lo-Cal-Wide-Noodles/" title="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles" alt="Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles">Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles</a></li>
  <li>Fresh coriander leaves for garnish</li>
  <li>Lime wedges for serving</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <h4>Preparing the Laksa Broth:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Heat up a medium size pot on high heat, once it's hot add vegetable oil and sear the chicken until browned and cook through and set aside.</li>
  <li>In the same pot add 2 tbsp vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Saut&eacute; for 2-3 minutes until fragrant on medium heat.</li>
  <li>Add the laksa paste to the pot and stir-fry for another 2 minutes to release its flavours.</li>
  <li>Pour in the vegetable broth and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavours to meld.</li>
  <li>Stir in the light coconut milk, soy sauce, and brown sugar (if using). Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.</li>
  <li>Season the broth with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Preparing the Laksa Toppings:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Remove Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles from the packet, rinse in cold running water for 15 seconds and drain well.</li>
  <li>Arrange the cooked chicken (or tofu), bean sprouts, and Chang's Super Lo-Cal Wide Noodles in serving bowls.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Assembling the Laksa:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Ladle the hot laksa broth over the prepared toppings in each bowl.</li>
  <li>Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with lime wedges.</li>
  </ol>
