ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>400g thick ling fillet (or a thick, firm white fish), skin and bones removed, cut into 3-4cm pieces</li>
  <li>400g salmon fillet, skin and bones removed, cut into 3-4cm pieces</li>
  <li>150 ml <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  <li>150g bag salad leaves</li>
  <li>2 tbl olive oil</li>
  <li>Pre-soaked wooden skewers</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Alternate the pieces of fish on the pre-soaked wooden or small metal skewers. Season with just a little sea salt and black pepper.</li>
  <li>Cook over medium/high heat on a lightly oiled flat grill, brushing 100ml of the <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a>.</li>
  <li>Toss the salad leaves with the remaining 50ml Oriental Salad Dressing and and the oil. Serve the salad with the grilled fish skewers.</li>
  </ol>
