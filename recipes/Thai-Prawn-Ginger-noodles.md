ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>100g Changs Traditional Super Low Cal noodles</li>
  <li>1&frac12;-2 tbsp red curry paste</li>
  <li>1-2 tsp Changs fish sauce</li>
  <li>2 tsp light brown soft sugar</li>
  <li>1 tbsp sunflower oil</li>
  <li>25g ginger</li>
  <li>2 large garlic cloves, sliced</li>
  <li>1 red pepper, deseeded and sliced</li>
  <li>85g sugar snap peas</li>
  <li>140g beansprouts</li>
  <li>175g pack raw king prawns</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Put the noodles in a bowl and pour over boiling water to cover them. Set aside to soak for 10 mins. Stir together the curry paste, fish sauce, sugar and 3 tbsp water to make a sauce.</li>
  <li>Heat the oil in a large wok and add half the ginger and the garlic. Cook, stirring, for 1 min. Add the pepper and stir-fry for 3 mins more. Toss in the sugar snaps, cook briefly, then pour in the curry sauce. Add the bean sprouts and prawns, and continue cooking until the prawns just turn pink.</li>
  <li>Drain the noodles, then toss these into the pan with the herbs and remaining ginger. Mix until the noodles are well coated in the sauce, then serve.</li>
  </ol>
