ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 whole chicken, excess fat removed</li>
  </ul>
  <h3>Marinade:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. C<a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a></li>
  <li>2 tbsp. honey</li>
  <li>1 tsp. Chinese five spice</li>
  <li>2 cloves garlic, finely grated or crushed</li>
  <li>1 tbsp. ginger, grated</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine all marinade ingredients into a saucepan and bring to the boil. Simmer contents for a minute to dissolve the honey, then allow to cool.</li>
  <li>Place the chicken in a bowl and cover both inside and out with the marinade. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for 2-12 hours, ideally turning the chicken in the marinade occasionally.</li>
  <li>Bring the chicken back to room temperature and bake on a rack in a 200 degree oven for 60-70 minutes, brushing with the remaining marinade after 30 minutes.</li>
  <li>Check the progress of the chicken by inserting a skewer or point of a sharp knife into thigh; if the juices run clear, it is cooked, if they are still rosy, cook for another 10 minutes.</li>
  <li>Serve marinated chicken with steamed Chinese broccoli or other Asian green vegetables.</li>
  </ol>
