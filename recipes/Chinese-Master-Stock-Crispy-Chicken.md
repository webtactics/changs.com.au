ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 x 500ml <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a></li>
  <li>500ml cold water</li>
  <li>1.6kg free range chicken, cut into 8 pieces</li>
  <li>500ml sunflower oil</li>
  <li>1 x 250g packet <a href="/products/Changs-Long-Life-Noodles/" title="Chang's Long Life Noodles" alt="Chang's Long Life Noodles">Chang's Long Life Noodles</a>, cooked according to directions on the packet</li>
  <li>1 cup loosely packed mint leaves, torn</li>
  <li>1 cup loosely packed coriander leaves</li>
  <li>1 spring onions, thinly sliced</li>
  <li>1 large carrot, sliced into matchsticks</li>
  <li>1/3 cup toasted cashews, roughly chopped</li>
  <li>1/4 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Pour <a href="/products/Changs-Master-Stock/" title="Chang's Master Stock" alt="Chang's Master Stock">Chang's Master Stock</a> and water into a large saucepan or stockpot and bring to the boil over high heat.</li>
  <li>Add chicken and cover. Simmer over medium heat for 20 minutes.</li>
  <li>Turn heat off and let chicken sit in the stock for one hour, covered.</li>
  <li>Remove chicken from stock and place on a paper towel lined tray for 30 minutes to dry the skin.</li>
  <li>Pour oil into large wok or saucepan and heat to 180 degrees.</li>
  <li>Place chicken pieces, skin side up carefully into the hot oil.</li>
  <li>Fry, ladling the hot oil over the top of the chicken for four-to-five minutes or until chicken is crisp and golden.</li>
  <li>Combine remaining ingredients in a large bowl and serve with the crispy skinned chicken.</li>
  </ol>
