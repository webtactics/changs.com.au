ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 kg meaty pork spare ribs</li>
  <li>2 cups water</li>
  <li>1 thumb of ginger, sliced</li>
  <li>1 150 ml bottle of <a href="/products/Changs-Japanese-Teriyaki-Sauce/" title="Chang's Japanese Teriyaki Sauce" alt="Chang's Japanese Teriyaki Sauce">Chang's Japanese Teriyaki Sauce</a> or Chang’s Hoisin Sauce</li>
  <li>3 gloves garlic, finely chopped</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. chopped ginger</li>
  <li>2 tbsp. brown sugar</li>
  <li>A few very thin slices or matchsticks of ginger, as garnish</li>
  <li>2 spring onions, sliced diagonally, as garnish</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Cut ribs into 6cm pieces and place in a large saucepan, cover with 2 cups of water and sliced ginger.</li>
  <li>Bring water to simmering point and simmer for 15 mins. Drain and rinse under cold water, discarding the ginger.</li>
  <li>Put ribs in a large bowl, combine <a href="/products/Changs-Japanese-Teriyaki-Sauce/" title="Chang's Japanese Teriyaki Sauce" alt="Chang's Japanese Teriyaki Sauce">Chang's Japanese Teriyaki Sauce</a>, garlic, ginger and sugar and pour mixture over ribs to cover. Place the ribs in a baking dish, add ½ cup of water and bake at 180 degrees for 40 mins.</li>
  <li>Serve hot sprinkled with ginger and spring onions, and freshly steamed rice.</li>
  </ol>
  <p>Tip: Ribs can be marinated in the sauce overnight (covered, in the fridge) before simmering for a more intense flavour.</p>
