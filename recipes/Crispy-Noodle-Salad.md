ingredients: |
  <p>This salad has an interesting Asian flavour combined with the crunch of Fried noodles and nuts.</p>
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> <strong>*</strong></li>
  <li>&frac12; Chinese cabbage (wombok), shredded finely</li>
  <li>6 green onions, sliced</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup slivered almonds, toasted</li>
  <li>1/2 cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  </ul>
  <p><strong>*</strong>alternatively you can use <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a> or <a href="/products/Changs-Crunchy-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles">Chang's Crunchy Fried Noodles</a></p>
  <p>Whilst we have made things easy with our <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a> you could also make your own dressing.</p>
  <h3>Dressing:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>&frac14; cup white vinegar</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup castor sugar</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>2 tsp <a href="/products/Changs-Sesame-Oil/" title="Chang's Sesame Oil" alt="Chang's Sesame Oil">Chang's Sesame Oil</a> (optional)</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup olive oil</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Finely shred the cabbage and place in a large bowl along with the <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a>, spring onions and almonds. Toss until combined.</li>
  <li>Just before serving pour <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a> over salad and toss until salad is fully coated.</li>
  <li>To make a delicious meal add 300g shredded BBQ chicken or BBQ pork.</li>
  </ol>
  <h3>The Best Way To Shred Cabbage</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Cut the cabbage in half, remove the thick white core section from the centre of each half. To do this, lay cabbage flat side down on the board and cut a &ldquo;v&rdquo; in the centre. Turn it over and ease the core out.</li>
  <li>Using a large, sharp knife, cut the cabbage into very fine shreds, working from one end. Regular cabbage tends to have tighter leaves so its best to shred it into one piece. Hold the cabbage firmly with one hand and shave down one side.</li>
  </ol>
