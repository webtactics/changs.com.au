ingredients: |
  <p>A delicious "eat the rainbow" twist on our Crispy Noodle Salad turned into an easy, no-cook salmon salad dinner in under 20.</p>
  <p>Perfect for mid-week dinners, but it's also a great number for entertaining served to be shared.</p>
  <p>While hot smoked salmon is our first choice for this dish, roasted shredded chicken or pulled pork would also go well here as they all taste great with hot sauce and <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a>.</p>
  <p>To make the meal gluten-free you can use <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a> .</p>
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>&frac14; small purple cabbage</li>
  <li>3 sprigs coriander</li>
  <li>2 spring onions</li>
  <li>1 packet <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a> or <a href="/products/Changs-Gluten-Free-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles">Chang's Gluten Free Fried Noodles</a> for a gluten-free option)</li>
  <li>&frac12; cup <a href="/products/Changs-Crispy-Noodle-Salad-Dressing/" title="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing" alt="Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing">Chang's Crispy Noodle Salad Dressing</a></li>
  <li>150g packet hot-smoked salmon* (we used pepper-crusted)</li>
  <li>3 tbsp hot sauce</li>
  <li>1 tsp sesame seeds</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Finely shred cabbage using a knife or a mandolin slicer. Place in a bowl. Chop coriander and spring onions and add to the bowl. Add Chang&rsquo;s Fried Noodles and Salad Dressing and mix well to coat.</li>
  <li>Divide half the salad between 2 serving plates (store the rest of the salad in an air-tight container in the fridge for up to 24 hours).</li>
  <li>Top with hot-smoked salmon and drizzle each with Chan&rsquo;s Hot Chilli Sauce. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve.</li>
  </ol>
  <p><em>*You can purchase a single pack of two smaller hot-smoked salmon fillets or use a larger single pack and simply crumble the salmon on top, dividing between two plates.</em></p>
