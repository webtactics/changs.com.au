ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>400g milk chocolate, melted</li>
  <li>3 packets <a title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" href="products/Original-Fried-Noodles/">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>1 packet M&amp;M&rsquo;s</li>
  <li>Writing Icing Pens in red and orange to decorate</li>
  <li>Icing sugar to dust</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Line a large baking tray with baking paper and set aside.</li>
  <li>Combine melted chocolate and noodles; mix until well combined. Form 2 heaped tablespoons of mixture into a mound onto prepared tray, repeat with remaining mixture.</li>
  <li>Refrigerate, uncovered, for 30 minutes or until firm.</li>
  <li>Use the writing icing pens to stick 3 M&amp;M&rsquo;s in a row on each mound, forming a row of eyes.</li>
  <li>Draw a squiggly line underneath the eyes as a mouth.</li>
  <li>Dust lightly with icing sugar if desired.</li>
  </ol>
