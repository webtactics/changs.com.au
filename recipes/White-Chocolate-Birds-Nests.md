ingredients: |
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <ul>
  <li>2 x 200g blocks of white chocolate, melted</li>
  <li>3 packets <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fried-Noodles/" title="Chang's Original Fried Noodles" alt="Chang's Original Fried Noodles">Chang's Original Fried Noodles</a></li>
  <li>2/3 cup Hundreds and Thousands</li>
  <li>10 paper texas muffin cases</li>
  <li>30 assorted mini Easter eggs</li>
  <li>Icing sugar to dust</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <ol>
  <li>Combine chocolate, noodles and Hundreds and Thousands in a medium bowl. Divide mixture amongst paper cases.</li>
  <li>Using a teaspoon, press into the centre of the mixture to make a deep indent.</li>
  <li>Place cases in the fridge for 30 minutes, or until firm. Remove the nests from the paper cases.</li>
  <li>Place three Easter eggs into each nest, and dust lightly with icing sugar.</li>
  </ol>
