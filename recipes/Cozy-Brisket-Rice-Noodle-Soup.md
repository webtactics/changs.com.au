ingredients: |
  <p>A deeply comforting, slow-cooked bowl with tender brisket, fragrant broth, and silky noodles.</p>
  <p>Finished with fresh herbs and a rich soy-cured egg, this is the kind of soup that feels like a warm hug.</p>
  <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
  <h4>Soup Base:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>700g beef brisket</li>
  <li>1 brown onion, peeled and halved</li>
  <li>4 garlic cloves, smashed</li>
  <li>1 tsp whole black peppercorns</li>
  <li>2 litres water</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Fish-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Fish Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Fish Sauce">Chang's Original Fish Sauce</a></li>
  <li>Spring onion, chopped (for serving)</li>
  <li>Coriander, chopped (for serving)</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Seasoned Brisket:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>Cooked brisket (reserved from broth)</li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  <li>1 tbsp <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a></li>
  <li>Freshly cracked black pepper</li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Soy-Cured Eggs:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li>2&ndash;4 eggs</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup mirin</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup cooking sake</li>
  <li>&frac14; cup <a href="/products/Changs-Tamari-Light-Soy-Sauce/" title="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce" alt="Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce">Chang's Gluten Free Tamari Light Soy Sauce</a></li>
  </ul>
  <h4>Noodles:</h4>
  <ul>
  <li><a href="/products/Changs-Vermicelli-Rice-Noodles/" title="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles" alt="Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles">Chang's Vermicelli Rice Noodles</a> (1 bundle per bowl)</li>
  </ul>
method: |
  <h3>Method:</h3>
  <h4>Soup Base:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Soak the brisket in a mixture of 1 tbsp white vinegar and enough cold water to cover. Leave for 30 minutes to 1 hour.</li>
  <li>Drain, rinse, and slice the brisket into four pieces.</li>
  <li>Place the brisket, onion, garlic, and peppercorns into a pressure cooker.</li>
  <li>Cover with water.</li>
  <li>Cook on high pressure for 45 minutes.</li>
  <li>Allow the pressure to release naturally.</li>
  <li>Remove the brisket and set aside on a tray.</li>
  <li>Strain the broth and return it to the pot.</li>
  <li>Season with Chang&rsquo;s Fish Sauce.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Soup Base -Shortcut Option:</h4>
  <p>Use a good quality store-bought beef broth.</p>
  <p>Simmer gently with onion, garlic, and peppercorns for 15&ndash;20 minutes.</p>
  <p>Strain and season with Chang&rsquo;s Fish Sauce.</p>
  <h4>Seasoned Brisket:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Shred the brisket into strips using two forks.</li>
  <li>Season with Chang&rsquo;s Tamari Light Soy Sauce, <a href="/products/Changs-Original-Oyster-Sauce/" title="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce" alt="Chang's Original Oyster Sauce">Chang's Original Oyster Sauce</a>, and black pepper.</li>
  <li>Toss gently to coat.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Soy-Cured Eggs:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Bring the eggs to a boil and cook for 6&frac12;&ndash;7 minutes for a jammy centre.</li>
  <li>Transfer immediately to ice water, then peel.</li>
  <li>Combine mirin, sake, and tamari in a bowl.</li>
  <li>Submerge the eggs and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.</li>
  <li>Slice in half before serving.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>Noodles:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Prepare according to packet instructions.</li>
  <li>Drain and divide between serving bowls.</li>
  </ol>
  <h4>To Assemble:</h4>
  <ol>
  <li>Place rice noodles into serving bowls.</li>
  <li>Ladle over the hot broth.</li>
  <li>Top with shredded brisket.</li>
  <li>Add half a soy-cured egg to each bowl.</li>
  <li>Finish with spring onion and coriander.</li>
  <li>Serve immediately.</li>
  </ol>
